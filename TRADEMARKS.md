Provided that you do not create confusion about source or imply endorsement, you may make truthful, descriptive references such as:

If you distribute a modified version, enterprise-customized build, derivative implementation, or compatible implementation, please follow these rules:

1. You may use factual phrases such as "based on EnClaws", "derived from EnClaws", or "compatible with EnClaws".
2. You should not name your modified distribution simply **EnClaws** or **企业龙虾** unless you are distributing the unmodified official version and your use will not create source confusion.
3. If your version materially differs from the official project, you should replace or remove official logos, icons, splash screens, banners, and similar branding elements that are likely to create confusion.
4. A naming pattern such as "YourBrand for EnClaws" or "YourBrand based on EnClaws" is generally better than using EnClaws as your primary brand.
